Farrow Entertainment is an independent label and management group based in Nashville, TN. Founded on the principle of getting artists we believe in heard and properly represented at crucial points in their development; Farrow continues to put their artists first. As a family of professionls committed to helping our artist's vision become a reality, we are relentless in our pursuit of excellence.
